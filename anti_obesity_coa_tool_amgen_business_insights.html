Market Access Value Story

These themes are built from the business impact slide you shared and translated into a launch-tool view.

Revenue & Pricing Impact

Use COA plus functional improvement to support willingness-to-pay, parity or premium positioning, and defense against rapid price erosion in a crowded GLP-1 market.

Market Access

Frame the asset around payer-relevant endpoints such as function, adherence, and QoL to support faster coverage decisions and lower friction around step therapy or prior authorization.

Value-Based Contracts

Tie outcomes to activity levels, adherence, durability, and patient functioning to make outcomes-based agreements more practical.

Differentiation Beyond “Weight Loss”

Many obesity stories stop at kilograms lost. This tool lets a launch team argue for broader patient value using longitudinal COA and durability evidence.

Cost-Effectiveness Story

Combine weight loss, HbA1c improvement, and patient functioning to model downstream reduction in progression to diabetes and broader utilization burden.

Adherence & Persistence

Identify early drop-off risk, slow responders, and plateau-with-low-COA segments so support programs can intervene earlier.
